## My Equipment

I currently use two main devices for my photography work. My Sony + Tamron 28-75mm is a compact full-frame mirrorless camera that delivers exceptional image quality while maintaining portability - it's my go-to for more serious photography projects, offering excellent low-light performance and versatility. For everyday photography and spontaneous moments, my Google Pixel 8 Pro provides computational photography capabilities that continue to impress with its AI-enhanced features, making it possible to capture stunning images on the go.
