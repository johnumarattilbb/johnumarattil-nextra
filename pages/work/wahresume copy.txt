## 💭 Lessons Learned: What I'd Do Differently

### 🎯 Key Insights

**1. Start with the Problem, Not the Solution**
Initially, I was obsessed with AI capabilities. The breakthrough came when I focused on user pain points first.

**2. Test Early, Test Often**
Every assumption I made was wrong until tested with real users. User feedback shaped 80% of the final product.

**3. Simplicity Wins**
The most successful features were the simplest ones. Complexity is the enemy of adoption.

**4. Trust is Everything**
In the career space, trust is paramount. Every design decision had to reinforce credibility and competence.
