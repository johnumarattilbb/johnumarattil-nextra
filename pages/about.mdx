# I'm Johnu Marattil

I'm a Product Designer currently employed at Harrison Healthcare. I have a passion for 
solving problems. My joy comes from applying design thinking methodology to transform complex 
user journeys into easily comprehensible user flows in products.  I excel in user research, 
copywriting, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, interaction design, and usability testing. 
Additionally, I excel in technologies like React, NextJs, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, 
WebGL, Lottie, Rive animations, and have proficiency in both mobile and web development.

During my tenure at CloudSEK from 2019 to 2022, I led design projects for XVigil and 
BeVigil, making significant strides in cybersecurity. BeVigil saw over 5k downloads, while 
XVigil earned a prestigious 5-star Gartner peer rating in 2021. My responsibilities included 
team management, enhancing product satisfaction, identifying improvement opportunities, and adapting designs 
to meet evolving customer preferences. I also designed CloudSEK's logo, symbolizing the 
company's commitment to AI-powered Digital Risk Monitoring solutions. Starting as a UX 
Designer in 2019, I progressed to Lead UX Designer in 2021 and received the Cloudster award by CloudSEK.

Before that, I was a Software Engineer at Vanenburg Software from 2017 to 2019.
During this time, my focus was on developing full stack apps specializing UIs for hybrid web and mobile applications.
I contributed to projects such as the Maqqie app and the Nebraska Department of Natural Resources app, emphasizing engaging and functional design solutions with responsive interfaces.

My academic background includes graduating from MG University, Kottayam in June 2017 with a
Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science majoring software engineering and minoring graphic design This 
education equipped me with a strong foundation in technical and design principles, enabling 
me to effectively merge engineering with user-centric design methodologies.

Driven by a passion for creating meaningful connections through design, I actively share 
insights on user research case studies and innovative solutions on platforms like LinkedIn 
and Medium.
